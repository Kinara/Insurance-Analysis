## üéØ Project Overview
This project presents a multi-tool analysis focused on key sales and operational metrics within an insurance institution. The primary objective is to provide deep insights into sales team performance, opportunity tracking, and revenue generation stages, enabling data-driven management of the sales cycle.

**This project showcases proficiency across major BI platforms:** Power BI, Tableau, and Excel.

---

## üìà Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) Analyzed

The dashboards were built around the following essential sales and revenue KPIs:

1.  **No of Invoice by Account Executive:** Tracking individual sales volume.
2.  **Yearly Meeting Count:** Monitoring sales activity and engagement over time.
3.  **Cross-Sell & Renewal Performance:** Detailed analysis comparing **Target, Achieved, and New** figures for:
    * Cross-Sell opportunities.
    * New customer acquisition.
    * Policy Renewal performance.
4.  **Stage Funnel by Revenue:** Visualization of the sales pipeline (funnel) based on revenue value to identify conversion bottlenecks.
5.  **No of Meeting By the Account Executive:** Assessing individual sales team engagement.
6.  **Top Open Opportunity:** Identifying and prioritizing high-value, active sales opportunities.
